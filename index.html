The Contranym Codex

Some words contain their own opposites — seeds of contradiction folded into a single form.

A contranym (also called an auto-antonym or Janus word) is a word with two definitions that directly contradict each other. Sanction can mean to approve or to penalise. Cleave means both to cling together and to split apart. Dust can mean to add a fine layer or to remove one.

This codex collects them — each entry laid out so both meanings face each other at once.
