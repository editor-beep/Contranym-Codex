The Contranym Codex
Codex Essay

A lexical cabinet of curiosities

One word.
Two truths.
No loyalty.

A contranym is a word that contains its own opposite. Sanction blesses and punishes. Cleave splits and clings. Bound escapes and is imprisoned. These are Janus words — double-faced, loyal to nothing.

CLEAVE to split apart — to sever Meaning I
CLEAVE to cling — to hold fast Meaning II
