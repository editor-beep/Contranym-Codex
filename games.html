THE GAMES ROOM

Enter if you dare. Language is already gaslighting you — these games just make it explicit.

Game I

THE FLIP GAME

Seven rounds. One word, two meanings. Identify which reading is operating — but be warned: sometimes it's both, and the language is laughing at you.

Game II

BOUND / UNBOUND

A Lynchian text escape room. Every object is a contranym. Every door is ambiguous. You have been here before. You will be here again.

Game III

SCREEN TEST

A routine wellness check. Five questions. Give the right answers. Your internal monologue is your own concern and will not be recorded. Probably.

Game IV

THE REDACTION

Six official documents. One word in each has been redacted. Reveal the word. Then determine what the document means. Spoiler: the document supports both readings equally.
